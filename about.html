Meet Your Instructor

Sarah Thompson

RYT-500 Certified Yoga Instructor with over 12 years of teaching experience. Sarah discovered yoga during a transformative period in her life and has dedicated herself to sharing its healing benefits ever since.

Trained in Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin, and Prenatal Yoga, Sarah creates inclusive classes that welcome students of all levels and abilities.
